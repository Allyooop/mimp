Within the Medical Imaging and Medical Physics Directorate at Sheffield Teaching Hospitals we employ skilled staff who contribute to the diagnosis and treatment of patients in many different ways, across a wide range of healthcare settings.

As technology and treatments evolve, so do our staff and their skills. Training and development are of vital importance to our directorate and the organisation as a whole. Both Medical Imaging and Medical Physics have teams dedicated to training and developing current and future staff.

Medical Physics and Clinical Engineering

Within the Medical Physics and Clinical Engineering department we have many varied roles and skilled professionals offering cutting-edge treatments and services.

Our services are staffed with Medical Physicists, Clinical Engineers, Clinical Scientists, Clinical Technologists, Electronic Engineers, Mechanical Engineers, Healthcare Scientists, Healthcare Science Practitioners, Healthcare Science Associates/Assistants and support staff.

Each individual member of staff within Medical Physics and Clinical Engineering has specialist training which provides them with the appropriate skills and expertise to perform tasks such as:

  • maintain medical equipment
  • use radiation for diagnosis and treatment
  • manufacture bespoke surgical tools
  • advise on radiation protection
  • optimize magnetic resonance imaging sequences
  • perform nuclear medicine imaging
  • analyze a persons gait
  • build bespoke electrical equipment for clinical measurement

Our staff also conduct research and specialist training which lead to new techniques for the diagnosis and treatment of disease
