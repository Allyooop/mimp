Audiological Science

  1. Home
  2. Services
  3. Audiological Science

Audiological Science is responsible for treating people with hearing and balance difficulties and provides scientific and clinical input to Audiology Services in Sheffield, Chesterfield and North East Derbyshire. The department also includes the Sheffield Hearing and Learning Impairment Service which offers bespoke audiological assessment and management for adults with intellectual impairment.
